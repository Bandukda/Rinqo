At Rinqo, we take your privacy seriously. Any information you provide through our website, forms, or phone system is used solely to set up and operate your voice agent. We will never sell, share, or distribute your personal or business data to third parties.

We collect only the information you provide directly: your name, phone number, email address, and restaurant details. Call data processed through your Rinqo agent (transcripts, reservation details) is stored securely and only accessible to you.

Your information is used exclusively to: set up and configure your voice agent, send you call summaries and reports, communicate with you about your account, and improve our service. Nothing more.
